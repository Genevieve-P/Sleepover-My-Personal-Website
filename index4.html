Recs and More!

So, I have done a lot of things already and I plan to do a lot more in the future. I thought it would be fun to share some of my recs for things I have done, tried, or just like in general. I hope you find something new that you will like to try out — or just find things you've already tried and love!


✦ hover over a card to flip it and see why it's a must! ✦

Anime & Shows
Jujutsu Kaisen
Sorcerers, curses, and incredibly hype fights.
Jujutsu Kaisen
Action-packed supernatural anime with loveable characters and intense battles.
Nobara Kugisaki is literally from this show — if she's your fave, you NEED to watch her full story. Very MHA energy with higher stakes.
Amphibia
A girl teleported to a magical frog world.
Amphibia
Funny, heartfelt adventure about a girl finding herself in a frog kingdom.
Made by the same creator as The Owl House — same found-family magic and emotional gut-punches. A perfect next watch!
Books
Percy Jackson
Greek myths, a magical school, and a sarcastic hero.
Percy Jackson
A boy discovers he's the son of a Greek god and must save the world — multiple times.
Has the Hogwarts energy AND the Hunger Games stakes. Fast, funny, and full of heart. The perfect bridge series!
School for Good and Evil
A magical school for fairy tale heroes and villains.
School for Good and Evil
Two best friends sorted into opposite sides of a fairy-tale school — chaos ensues.
If you miss Hogwarts, this fills that gap perfectly. Dark, twisty, and full of strong female characters!
Games
Kirby: Forgotten Land
Kirby in a gorgeous 3D adventure world.
Kirby: Forgotten Land
Kirby explores a post-apocalyptic world, absorbing enemies and transforming into wild objects.
Your fave pink puffball gets his best game ever. Charming, gorgeous, and just the right difficulty!
Hi-Fi Rush
Everything — combat, world, story — happens on the beat.
Hi-Fi Rush
A rhythm-action game where the entire world pulses to the music.
Made for someone who loves Just Dance and pop music. The style is very anime-esque. Instant mood booster!
Music
NewJeans
Dreamy Y2K-tinged K-Pop with irresistible hooks.
NewJeans
A K-Pop group blending nostalgic 2000s sounds with fresh, effortless cool.
If you love K-Pop AND 2010s pop, NewJeans is the perfect overlap. Hype Boy and OMG are absolute earworms!
Yoasobi
Story-driven J-Pop that hits emotionally every time.
Yoasobi
A duo that turns novels into songs — every track tells a full emotional story.
They did the Beastars and Oshi no Ko openings. Best of anime AND J-Pop in one artist!
Food & Dessert
Mochi Ice Cream
Chewy Japanese rice dough filled with ice cream.
Mochi Ice Cream
Soft chewy mochi outside, cold creamy ice cream inside — tons of flavors!
Combines your love of ice cream with trying new foods. Strawberry and matcha are the best starting flavors!
Korean Street Sweets
Dalgona candy, bingsu, and honey butter chips.
Korean Street Sweets
A world of unique sweets — viral dalgona candy, shaved milk ice, and more.
Perfect for a K-Pop fan who loves candy and trying new things. Bingsu hits like a dream on a warm day!
Hobbies
Amigurumi Plushies
Crochet your own tiny Kirby or axolotl!
Amigurumi Plushies
Japanese-style crocheted stuffed animals — tiny, adorable, and satisfying to make.
Free Kirby, Kuromi, and axolotl patterns are all over Pinterest and Ravelry. Crochet your faves!
Fandom Art Challenges
Monthly events to level up and connect with artists.
Fandom Art Challenges
Community drawing events like Inktober and OC-tober where artists share daily themed work.
Improve your digital art and connect with other MHA and Owl House fans. The community is so supportive!
